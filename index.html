Why Choose Us

The personal injury law firm of Barrett Nonni & Homola combines Vinse Barrett’s 40 years of legal experience with the energetic and aggressive approach of Mark Nonni and Jaeson Homola. The result is legal representation with all the tools to not only win your case, but to win it quickly.

The personal injury attorneys at Barrett Nonni & Homola understand that the legal process can be frustrating and confusing. Our attorneys take pride in making themselves available to clients so questions and concerns can be addressed without delay. Direct communication between our attorneys and our clients not only eases our clients’ anxiety, but it also helps the attorneys better understand the facts of our clients’ cases, which assists in achieving the maximum recovery.
